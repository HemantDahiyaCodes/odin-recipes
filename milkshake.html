Recipe to make milkshake

Description:

here's a basic recipe for making a delicious milkshake:

Ingrediants:

  • 2 cups of vanilla ice cream (about 4 large scoops)
  • 1 cup of milk (adjust according to desired thickness)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional, for extra vanilla flavor)
  • Whipped cream (optional, for topping)
  • Maraschino cherry (optional, for garnish)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Blender: Make sure your blender is clean and ready to use.
  2. Add Ingredients: Add the vanilla ice cream, milk, and vanilla extract (if using) to the blender.
  3. Blend: Blend the ingredients on high speed until smooth and creamy. If the shake is too thick, add a little more milk and blend again until you reach the desired consistency.
  4. Serve: Pour the milkshake into a tall glass.
  5. Optional Toppings: Top with whipped cream and garnish with a maraschino cherry if desired.
  6. Enjoy: Insert a straw and enjoy your delicious vanilla milkshake!
